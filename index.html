About the Founder

Eneh Mercy is the founder of TEFEM, a social enterprise aimed at improving the living stanndard of ladies by empowering them with digital skills. Eneh Mercy is a brand identity designer and branding strategist. A digital creator with over 5 years of experience in photography and visual design.
